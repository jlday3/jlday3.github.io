About Inition Education Solution and its Mission

Our goal is to empower children around the world through education; whose right of receiving an education has been deprived due to harsh social environments and life obstacles. Inition strives to expand the access to education through the use, empowerment, and knowledge of technology.

Learning sciences can leverage socio economic conditions for underprivileged communities and serve as a catalyst for children of all genders to pursue degrees in science, technology, engineering, and math (STEM) areas.

Facts about current Education System

Over the past 4 decades, the United Staes has slipped from being a world leader in education to what appears to be a bunch of dropouts and under-achievers.
